HISTORY

Founded in 1869, La Puk National University (L.N.U) has grown from a small community college into a leading institution of higher learning. Guided by excellence, integrity, and service, the university is dedicated to producing competent professionals and leaders who contribute to national and global progress.

SPECIALTY

L.N.U is recognized for its strong programs in Information Technology, Teacher Education, and Business Administration, as well as its commitment to research and community development.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

Undergraduate Degrees: Computer Science, Information Technology, Business Administration, Education, Nursing, Engineering, Communication

Graduate Degrees: Information Technology, Business Administration, Education, Public Administration

Special Programs: Professional Teaching Certificate, Web Development, Data Analytics, English Proficiency
